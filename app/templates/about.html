About gtRPG

gtRPG is a web app which applies common RPG concepts to music practice and practice tracking. With gtRPG, users can choose Categories - musical genres or activities that the User wants to improve in - and create Tasks - practice items associated with a Category. Users can then complete tasks in practice Sessions to earn XP towards leveling up.
